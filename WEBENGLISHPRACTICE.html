Settings

Practice language

The language you want to practice. This sets speech recognition, the tutor's voice and the tutor's language.

Explanations language

Corrections and flashcard translations will be written in this language (your native language).

Conversation

Control how the tutor reacts while you are speaking.

1 means the tutor responds very quickly · 10 means it waits longer for you to finish speaking or thinking.

Your level & help

The app adapts to you. As a beginner the tutor uses simple words and explains in your language so you can understand.

Tutor formality

The register the AI uses when talking to you.

Microphone

If recognition is inaccurate, choose a better microphone (ideally a headset) and keep noise cancellation on.

AI engine (Ollama)

The app sends your messages to a local Ollama server. Set the URL and model.

Voice

The voice used to read words and the tutor's replies out loud.

Data

Your flashcards and settings are stored in this browser.
